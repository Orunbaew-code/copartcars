About Future Sale Date

Regarding Lots NOT assigned to an auction.

Vehicles with sale dates of “Future” are NOT assigned to an auction yet. All title processing required to sell these vehicles has been completed and submitted to the proper titling authority. Copart is waiting for DMV processing to be completed. When DMV processing is completed, these vehicles will be assigned to an actual sale. Members bidding on these vehicles will be notified by email when the vehicle is assigned to a sale.

About Upcoming Lots

Vehicles with a sale date of “Upcoming Lot” have NOT been assigned to an auction and are NOT eligible for bidding yet. Add this vehicle to your Watchlist and Copart will let you know when a sale date is assigned and when you can start bidding.
